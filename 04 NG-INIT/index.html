NG-INIT Directive in AngularJS

Example of ng-init directive to initialize variables in AngularJS.

  1. Name: {{name}}
  2. Age: {{age}}
  3. Gender: {{sex}}

Example of ng-init directive to initialize an object in AngularJS.

  1. Class: {{Student.Class}}
  2. Enrollment No: {{Student.RollNo}}
  3. College Name: {{Student.College}}

Example of ng-init directive to initialize an array in AngularJS.

  1. Array: {{fruits}}
  2. First Fruit: {{fruits[0]}}
  3. Second Fruit: {{fruits[1]}}
  4. Third Fruit: {{fruits[2]}}
